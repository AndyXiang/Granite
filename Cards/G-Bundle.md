Let $(E,B,F,\pi)$ be a [[Fiber Bundle|fiber bundle]] and $G$ be a [[Group|group]]. A **$G$-bundle** is $(E,B,F,\pi,G)$ with $G$ [[Group Action|acts]] on $F$ together with [[Morphism|morphisms]] $U_{i}\cap U_{j}\to G$ that, relative to the action, gives the [[Fiber Bundle#Transition functions|transition functions]], where $U_{i},U_{j}$ are open [[Covering Space|covering]].
