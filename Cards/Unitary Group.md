The **unitary group** of degree $n$ is the [[Lie Subgroup|Lie subgroup]] of the [[General Linear Group|general linear group]] over complex field $\mathrm{GL}(n,\mathbb{C})$ as
$$\mathrm{U}(n)=\{ u:u^{\dagger}u=1_{n\times n} \}.$$
The **special unitary group** is defined with extra condition
$$\mathrm{\mathrm{SU}}(n)=\{ u:u\in \mathrm{U}(n),\det(u)=1 \}.$$
One has the [[Homomorphism (Group)|isomorphism]]
$$\mathrm{U}(n)\cong \frac{\mathrm{U}(1)\times \mathrm{SU}(n)}{\mathbb{Z}_{n}}  .$$

## Properties
### Topology
1. $\mathrm{SU}(n)$ is [[Connected Space|simply connected]] and [[Compact Space|compact]].
2. $\mathrm{SU}(n)$ is a [[Simple Lie Group|simple Lie group]], meaning its [[Lie Algebra|Lie algebra]] $\mathfrak{su}(n)$ is [[Simple Lie Algebra|simple]].
