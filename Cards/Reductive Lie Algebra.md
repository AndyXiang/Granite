A [[Lie Algebra|Lie algebra]] $\mathfrak{g}$ is **reductive** if $\mathrm{rad}(\mathfrak{g})=Z(\mathfrak{g})$, where $\mathrm{rad}(\mathfrak{g})$ refers to the unique [[Solvable Lie Algebra|solvable]] [[Lie Ideal|ideal]] which contains any other solvable ideal, and $Z(\mathfrak{g})$ is the center of $\mathfrak{g}$.
