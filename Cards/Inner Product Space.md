**Inner product space** is a [[Vector Space|vector space]] $V$ defined with [[Field (Math)|field]] $\mathbb{K}$ equipped with an operation $(\cdot,\cdot):V\times V\to \mathbb{K}$, which is called the **inner product**, satisfying
1. *symmetric*: $\forall x,y\in V,(x,y)=(y,x)$.
2. linear: $\forall x,y,z\in V,a,b\in \mathbb{K},(ax+by,z)=a(x,z)+b(y,z)$.
3. positive-definite: $\forall x\in V-\{ 0 \}, (x,x)>0$.
