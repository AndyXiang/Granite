**G parity** is a [[Quantum Number|multiplicative quantum number]] derived from the combination of [[Charge Conjugation|C parity]] and [[Isospin|isospin]]. Explicitly the G operator is defined as
$$\hat{\mathcal{G}}=\hat{\mathcal{C}}\mathrm{e}^{ \mathrm{i}\pi \mathrm{I}_{2} }.$$

Since [[Strong Interaction|strong interaction]] conserves C parity and isospin, G parity is also conversed. Similarly, G parity is not conversed under [[Z Electromagnetic Interaction|electromagnetic interaction]] and [[Weak Interaction|weak interaction]].
