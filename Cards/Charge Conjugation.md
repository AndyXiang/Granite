**Charge conjugation** is an operation that transforms a [[Z Single Particle State|single particle state]] into the state of its antiparticle
$$\hat{\mathcal{C}}\ket{a}=\eta_{C}(a)\ket{\bar{a}},  $$
where $\bar{a}$ refers to its antiparticle. Here $\eta_{C}(a)$ is called **C phase**. More generally, for multi-particle state:
$$\hat{\mathcal{C}}\ket{ab\cdots}=\eta_{C}(a)\eta_{C}(b)\cdots\ket{\bar{a}\bar{b}\cdots}.  $$
A *neutral particle* is a particle that all [[Quantum Number|additive quantum number]] equal to zero. Such particle is eigenstate of C transformation. The eigenvalue of C transformation for these neutral particles is called **C parity** of them.
