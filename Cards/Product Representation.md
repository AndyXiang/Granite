Let $G$ be a [[Group|group]], $(\pi,V)$ and $(\rho,W)$ be two [[Linear Representation|linear representations]]. The **product representation** of these two is another representation $(\pi \otimes\rho,V\otimes W)$ as
$$(\pi \otimes \rho)(g)=\pi(g)\otimes \rho(g),$$
define with the [[Direct Product (Vector Space)|direct product of vector spaces]].

Generally this representation is not [[Irreducible Representation|irreducible representation]], as
$$\pi \otimes \rho \cong \sum_{\lambda\in \mathrm{irrep}(G)}c_{\pi\rho}^{\lambda}\lambda$$
where $\lambda$ is irreducible representation of $G$. The coefficients $c_{\pi\rho}^{\lambda}$ corresponds to [[Clebsch-Gordan Coefficients|Clebsch-Gordan coefficient]].
