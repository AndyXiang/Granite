Let $\mathfrak{g},\mathfrak{a}$ be two [[Lie Algebra|Lie algebras]], and $\rho :\mathfrak{g}\to \mathrm{aut}(\mathfrak{a})$ be a [[Lie Algebra|morphism]] of Lie algebras. The **semidirect product** of $\mathfrak{g}$ and $\mathfrak{a}$ with $\rho$ is the [[Direct Sum of Vector Space|direct sum of vector space]] $\mathfrak{g}\oplus \mathfrak{a}$ equipped with Lie brackets
$$[(x,t),(y,s)]=([x,y],[t,s]+\rho(x)(s)-\rho(y)(t)),$$
for $x,y\in \mathfrak{g},t,s\in \mathfrak{a}$. This is denoted by $\mathfrak{g}\rtimes_{\rho}\mathfrak{a}$.
