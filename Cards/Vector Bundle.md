A **vector bundle** is a [[Fiber Bundle|fiber bundle]] $(E,B,V,\pi)$ where $V$ is a [[Topological Vector Space|topological vector space]] and the local trivializations $\phi: \pi ^{-1}(U)\to U\times V$ induce [[Linear Transformation|linear transformation]] of $V$ when restricted to $x\in U$.

A **[[Morphism|morphism]] of vector bundles** is a [[Fiber Bundle#Morphism of fiber bundles|morphism of fiber bundles]]  that also induces linear transformation when restricted to each fiber.
