**Specht modules** are [[Linear Representation|linear representations]] of [[Permutation Group|permutation group]] $S_{n}$, hence [[Module|modules]] of the [[Group Algebra|group algebra]] $\mathbb{K}S_{n}$, which are indexed by the [[Partition|partitions]] of $n$. For partition $\lambda$, the Specht module is usually denoted by $S^{(\lambda)}$.

>[!tip] Irreducible Specht modules
>In [[Field|fields]] of $0$ [[Characteristic|characteristic]], Specht modules are [[Irreducible Representation|irreducible]] and exhaust the isomorphism classes of irreducible representations.
>
