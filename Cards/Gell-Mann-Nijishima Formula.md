The **Gell-Mann-Nijishima formula** states the relation of charge, [[Isospin|isospin]] and other additive [[Quantum Number|quantum numbers]]. Explicitly one has
$$Q=I_{3}+\frac{1}{2}(B+S+C+B'+T),$$
where $Q$ is the charge, $I_{3}$ is the third component of isospin, $B$ is [[Baryon|baryon number]], $S,C,B',T$ are strangeness, charm, bottomness and topness numbers.
