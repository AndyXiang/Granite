Let $(P,B,G,\pi)$ be a [[Principal Bundle|right principal bundle]] and $F$ be a [[Topological Space|topological space]]. Let $G$ [[Group Action|acts]] on $F$ on the left.  Define a [[Equivalence Relation|equivalence relation]] based on two action as $(pg,f)\sim(p,gf)$. The **associated bundle** $(P,F)$ is the according [[Equivalence Class|equivalence class]] $(P\times F) /\sim$.
