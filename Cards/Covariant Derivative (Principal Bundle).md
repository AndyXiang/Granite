Let $P(M,G)$ be a [[Principal Bundle|principal bundle]] on the [[Differential Manifold|manifold]] $M$. Let $\phi\in\Omega^{r}(P)\otimes V$ be a [[Vector Space|vector-value]] [[Exterior Differential Form|r-form]] on $P$. Let $\omega$ be a [[Principal Bundle#Connections on principal bundle|connection]] on $P$, which separates [[Tangent Space|tangent space]] $T_{p}P$ into $H_{p}P\oplus V_{p}P$. The **covariant derivative** of $\phi$ is a vector-value $(r+1)$-form as
$$D\phi(X_{1},\cdots,X_{r+1})=e_{\mu}\otimes  \mathrm{d}\phi^{\mu}(X_{1}^{H},\cdots,X_{r+1}^{H})$$
where $\{ e_{\mu} \}$ is the basis of $V$, $\mathrm{d}:\Omega^{r}(M)\to\Omega^{r+1}(M)$ is the [[Exterior Differential Form|exterior derivative]].
