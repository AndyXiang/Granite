**Tangent bundle** is a [[Vector Bundle|vector bundle]] $(E,M,TM,\pi)$ where $E$ and $M$ are [[Differential Manifold|differential manifold]], and $TM$ is a [[Tangent Space|tangent space]] of $M$. Here $\pi$ and local trivializations are [[Smooth Mapping|smooth mappings]]. 

The [[Fiber Bundle#Section|section]] of tangent bundle on $M$ is a **vector field** on $M$. The set of all vector fields is denoted by $\mathfrak{X}(M)$.
