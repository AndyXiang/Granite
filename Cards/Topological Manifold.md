A **manifold** is a [[Topological Space|topological space]] $X$ that look *locally* like a [[Cartesian Space|Cartesian space]], commonly a finite-dimensional one, and possibly be infinite-dimensional. 

## Definition
A **locally Euclidean space** is a topological space $(X,\tau)$ if every point $x\in X$ has an [[Neighborhood|open neighborhood]] $U_{x}$ which is [[Homeomorphism|homeomorphic]] to the Euclidean space $\mathbb{R}^{n}$ with its [[Z Metric Topology|metric topology]].

A **topological manifold** is a topological space that is both locally Euclidean and [[Paracompact Space|paracompact]] [[Hausdorff Topological Space|Hausdorff]].
