Let $E$ be a [[Inner Product Space|inner product space]] and $R$ be its [[Root Space|root space]]. The **Weyl group** $W$ of $R$ is the [[Subgroup|subgroup]] of [[General Linear Group|general linear group]] $\mathrm{GL}(E)$ generated by $s_{\alpha},\alpha\in R$.

## Properties
1. The Weyl group $W$ is a finite subgroup in the orthogonal group $\mathrm{O}(E)$.
2. The root space $R$ is invariant under the [[Group Action|action]] of W.
3. For any $w\in W,\alpha\in R$, we have $s_{w(\alpha)}=ws_{\alpha}w^{-1}$.
