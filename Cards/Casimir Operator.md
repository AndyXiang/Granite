Let $\mathfrak{g}$ be a [[Lie Algebra|Lie algebra]]. The elements in the [[Center|center]] of the [[Universal Enveloping Algebra|universal enveloping algebra]] $U(\mathfrak{g})$ are called **Casimir operators**.


## Examples
Let $B$ be the [[Killing Form|Killing form]] of $\mathfrak{g}$. The *rank-2 Casimir operator* is
$$C_{2}=B^{ij}x_{i}x_{j}$$
for $B^{ij}=B(x_{i},x_{j})$.
