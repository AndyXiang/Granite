Let $(P,M,G,\pi,G,\mu)$ be a [[Principal Bundle|principal bundle]] on the [[Differential Manifold|manifold]] $M$. The **curvature 2-[[Exterior Differential Form|form]]** is the [[Covariant Derivative (Principal Bundle)|covariant derivative]] of the [[Principal Bundle#Connections on principal bundle#Algebraic definition|connection 1-form]] 
$$\Omega=D\omega\in \Omega^{2}(P)\otimes \mathfrak{g}.$$

The curvature 2-form satisfies
1. $R_{g}^{*}\Omega=g^{-1}\Omega g,g\in G$.
2. $\Omega(X,Y)=\mathrm{d}\omega(X,Y)+[\omega(X),\omega(Y)],X,Y\in TP$.
3. [[Bianchi Identity|Bianchi identity]]: $D\Omega=0$
